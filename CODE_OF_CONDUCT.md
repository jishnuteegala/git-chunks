# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex
characteristics, gender identity and expression, experience level, education,
socioeconomic status, nationality, appearance, race, caste, color, religion,
or sexual identity and orientation.

We will act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming,
diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and specific.
- Assume good intent while accepting correction.
- Focus criticism on ideas and code, not people.
- Respect differing viewpoints, boundaries, and privacy.
- Accept responsibility, apologize, and learn from mistakes.

Harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized attention, trolling, personal
attacks, and publishing another person's private information are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, pull
requests, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct. They may
temporarily or permanently restrict participation for inappropriate behavior.

Report conduct concerns privately through the maintainer contact options on
the [GitHub profile](https://github.com/jishnuteegala). Reports will be handled
as confidentially and promptly as possible. Do not open a public issue about
another person's conduct.
